## Research Philosophy

1. Systems research [must be humanistic](https://systemexplorers.substack.com/p/humanistic-systems-research) – ***humans are not machines***.
2. Systems research should effectively ***synthesize holistic and reductionist perspectives*** while maintaining rigor and flexibility.
3. Systems researchers should ***appreciate the philosophical and theoretical insights of early pioneers*** while making use of modern computational techniques and scientific advances.
4. Systems researchers should aim to ***solve practical transdisciplinary problems*** to help inform the process of theoretical development.
