Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) are a novel form of internet-based
organization. If you are a member of any internet communities, it's worth taking
some time to learn about them. 

DAOs differentiate themselves from the online communities we're used to (ie:
subreddits, Twitter sub-graphs, Facebook groups) by giving their members a
built-in mechanism for pooling and deploying funds to achieve shared goals.
